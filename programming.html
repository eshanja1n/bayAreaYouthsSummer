Introduction to Programming


In this introductory course to programming, students will be exposed to the fundamentals of programming using Java. They will explore the basics of object-oriented programming and will have a chance to write their own programs. All instructors are highly qualified high school students who have years of experience with coding.
At the end of this course, students will learn:

  • Overview
  • Basic Computer Concepts
  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Object-Oriented-Programming
  • Hello World Program
